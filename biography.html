Written by
J.K. Rowling
Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them (often referred to as simply Fantastic Beasts) is a 2001 guide book written by British author J. K. Rowling (under the pen name of the fictitious author Newt Scamander) about the magical creatures in the Harry Potter universe.












Directed by
David Yates
Yates directed the final four films in the Harry Potter series. His work on the series brought him critical and commercial success along with accolades, such as the British Academy Britannia Award for Excellence in Directing. Yates's subsequent projects include The Legend of Tarzan
Produced by
David Heyman
English film producer and the founder of Heyday Films. In 1999, he secured the film rights to the Harry Potter film series and went on to produce all eight installments. Heyman has received three Academy Award for Best Picture nominations for Gravity, Once Upon a Time in Hollywood and Marriage Story.












Produced by
Steve Kloves
an American screenwriter, film director and producer. He wrote and directed the 1989 film The Fabulous Baker Boys and is mainly known for his adaptations of novels, especially for all but one of the Harry Potter films and for Wonder Boys.
Produced by
Lionel Wigram
a British film producer and screenwriter. He is the grandson of the war hero Lionel Wigram












Music by
James N Howard
American composer, conductor, and music producer. He has scored over 100 films and is the recipient of a Grammy Award, Emmy Award, and eight Academy Award nominations.
